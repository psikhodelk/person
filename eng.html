📊 Грамматика & Маркеры времени

Present Simple vs Continuous:
• Simple (постоянно): V/Vs. usually, every day, often
• Continuous (сейчас): am/is/are + Ving. currently, at the moment, now
Past Simple vs Present Perfect:
• Past Simple (дата): V2/-ed. yesterday, in 2020, ago, last week
• Present Perfect (результат): have/has + V3. just, already, yet, so far, since
Conditionals (Условия):
• 1st (Реально): If + Present Simple, WILL + V1. (If we lower the price, sales will rise).
• 2nd (Мечта): If + Past Simple (were), WOULD + V1. (If I were you, I would invest).
Passive Voice: am/is/are/was/were + V3/ed. (The report was written yesterday).
