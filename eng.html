📊 Грамматика: Времена и Условия

1. Present Simple vs Continuous:
• Simple (постоянно): V / V-s. Маркеры: usually, every day, often, always.
• Continuous (сейчас): am/is/are + V-ing. Маркеры: currently, at the moment, now, this month.
2. Past Simple vs Present Perfect:
• Past Simple (точка в прошлом): V2 / -ed. Маркеры: yesterday, last year, in 2020, ago.
• Present Perfect (связь с настоящим): have/has + V3. Маркеры: just, already, yet, since, for, so far.
3. Conditionals (Условные предложения):
• Type 1 (Реально): If + Present Simple, WILL + V1. (If we lower prices, sales will rise).
• Type 2 (Маловероятно): If + Past Simple, WOULD + V1. (If I were you, I would invest). *NB: If I were (не was)!
4. The Passive (Пассив):
• Формула: be + V3/ed. (The report is written. The car was launched yesterday).
