3. Грамматическая шпаргалка

Present Simple vs Continuous:
• Simple (всегда): "We sell cars." (Маркеры: usually, every day).
• Continuous (сейчас): "We are selling more now." (Маркеры: currently, at the moment).
Conditionals (Условные):
• Type 1 (Real): If + Present, WILL + глагол (If we cut prices, we will sell more).
• Type 2 (Unreal): If + Past (were), WOULD + глагол (If I were you, I would quit).
