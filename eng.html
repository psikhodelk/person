📊 Грамматика

Present Simple vs Continuous: usually, every day vs currently, now.
Past Simple vs Present Perfect: yesterday, in 2020 vs just, already, since.
Conditionals: 1st: If + Present, Will. 2nd: If + Past (were), Would.
